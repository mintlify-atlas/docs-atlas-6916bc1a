Utilities for handling timestamps, time zones, date calculations, and data analysis tasks. All tools run client-side in your browser.

## Time and Date Tools

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Timestamp Converter"
    icon="calendar-clock"
    href="https://tools.simonwillison.net/unix-timestamp"
  >
    Convert Unix timestamps to readable dates in UTC and local timezone
  </Card>
  
  <Card
    title="Timezones"
    icon="earth-americas"
    href="https://tools.simonwillison.net/timezones"
  >
    Compare current times across multiple time zones simultaneously
  </Card>
  
  <Card
    title="Date Calculator"
    icon="calculator"
    href="https://tools.simonwillison.net/date-calculator"
  >
    Count days between dates or calculate only weekdays
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The Timestamp Converter automatically detects whether your input is a 10-digit (seconds) or 13-digit (milliseconds) Unix timestamp.
</Note>
